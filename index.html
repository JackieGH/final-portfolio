About Me

Jackie (she/her/they/them)

Hello! I'm Jackie, a second-year, Math-Computer Science major
at the University of California, San Diego. My favorite thing about computer science is that there are endless ways to incorporate coding in our daily lives. Coding isn't just for nerds! Or, incognito hackers... Coding can be fun and lighthearted! I work with multiple mediums to bring pop-culture moments from my childhood to life. While not every project proves to be a stroke of genius, learning is always better when it's fun!

My aspirations for the future include sharing what I know to encourage others to explore the world of coding, software, and web development. A lot of my inspiration comes from recreating the cool-tech things I saw on tv shows growing up. I'm pretty sure I'm not the only one who wanted a "pear phone" growing up. And, far before that, I remember watching my cousin on her computer and wishing I was old enough to make a "Myspace"! A lot has definitely changed since then, but I'm so excited that now I can do the cool tech things I always wanted to. When I'm not debugging code, I like to be out exploring, skating, or spending time with family. But, more often than not, I'm at home cooking and trying new recipes on my wife.
